Empanadas

Description

Ingredients

  • Empanada lids
  • Cow or chicken meat
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the stuffing of the empanadas, cooking and incorporating the ingredients
  2. Stuff the lids with the preparation made in point 1
  3. Cook the empanadas, it can be made by frying them or heating them in the oven
