Milanesa

Description

Ingredients

  • Cow meat, preferably rump, or chicken breast
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Provencal

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat in a preparation of eggs, provencal, salt and pepper
  2. Cover the meat with the bread crumbs
  3. Cook by oven or frying it
